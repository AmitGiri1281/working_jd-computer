About Our Institute

Quality computer education since 2010 in Jaunpur

Our Story

J.D. Computer Education was established in 2010 under the J.D. Educational Welfare Society with the mission to provide quality computer education to students in Jaunpur and surrounding areas.

We are proud to be affiliated with NIELIT (formerly DOEACC) and offer government-recognized courses that provide excellent career opportunities to our students.

Over the years, we have trained thousands of students who are now successfully working in government and private sector jobs across India.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality computer education with practical training that prepares students for real-world challenges in the IT industry.

Our Vision

To become the premier computer education center in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, recognized for excellence in training and student placements.
